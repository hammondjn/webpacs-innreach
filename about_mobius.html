MOBIUS is a consortium of libraries in the state of Missouri. The membership includes 62 full members from colleges, universities, the Missouri State Library, and 2 public libraries (cooperating partners).

The largest single project for which MOBIUS is responsible is The Common Library Platform project. The CLP creates an online union catalog of approximately 18 million items and creates a single user interface that allows users at member institutions to request library materials using any personal computer in any location with access to the Internet. Requested materials are delivered within one or two days of being requested by the MOBIUS Delivery System.

MOBIUS is responsible for other projects including consortial licensing of electronic information resources such as electronic journals, cooperative collection development, and a cooperative storage facility. MOBIUS is supported entirely by member fees.

The MOBIUS Consortium Office is located at the University of Missouri in Columbia, Missouri.
